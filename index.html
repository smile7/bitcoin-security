Introduction

This coursework introduces you to the basics of the 'Blockchain' technology. It gives you fundamental knowledge and helps you get on the road of blokchain. Various topics are covered - from 'Introduction to Blockchain' and 'Usage of Blockchain' to Bitcoin in details as the major cryptocurrency that depends on Blockchain. You will learn what is Bitcoin, how to use it and how to protect your investments by protecting your privacy and Internet data.
