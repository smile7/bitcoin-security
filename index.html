Conclusion

After all this information said, bitcoins can be classified as secure. Yes, they can be stolen. But this can happen only if the person, owning them, allows this. If one acts responsible for his tokens and creates the right storing conditions, he will not have to worry about losing them.

And in the end, if they weren't secure, they wouldn't have been so popular.

BUT, there are some potential risks like quantum computers, which are under development at the moment. The digital computers, we use now, require data to be encoded into binary digits (bits), each of which is always in one of two definite states (0 or 1). As Wikipedia states, quantum computation uses quantum bits, which can be in superpositions of states. This speeds up all processes and thus threatening the existence of Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies.
