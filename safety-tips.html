Safety Tips

As there exist some vulnarabilites and ways bitcoins can be stolen, if you take the correct actions, your coins will be secure. You just need to familirise yourself with what you should do to avoid putting your wallet at risk. Here are some of the ways, hackers can get to you:

  • Someone hacks your account

    • What happens?

    If you use a third-party service, you don’t have to remember your public and private key. Instead, you only remember your username and password, as in every other website. But this makes it possible for thieves to rob you by obtaining your password. The most common way they do this is by breaking into customers’ email accounts, and then asking the service you’re using to reset their password. The password reset request is then sent to the compromised email account, allowing the thief access to the bitcoin funds.

    How to prevent it?

    Secure your email account and bitcoin storage service you use with 2-factor authentication - for example login that consists not only with password authentication, but SMS also. You may also use Google Authenticator.
